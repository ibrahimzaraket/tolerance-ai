Residence Time Distribution

Graphical method for determining experimental residence time using tracer injection. Enter your time & conductivity data, flow rate, and reactor volume to compute τexp = Surface2 / Surface1 and compare with τth = V / Q.

Method

Principle: Inject a tracer at t=0 and follow the evolution of its concentration C(t) (in μS) at the exit over time t (in seconds).
Surface 1 = ∑((tn+1 − tn) × C(tn)) — area under C(t) curve (rectangle method)
Surface 2 = ∑((tn+1 − tn) × C(tn) × tn) — area under t×C(t) curve (rectangle method)
τexperimental = Surface 2 / Surface 1
τtheoretical = V / Q

Input Data

Enter matching pairs: the first time value corresponds to the first conductivity value, etc. Both lists must have the same number of values.
